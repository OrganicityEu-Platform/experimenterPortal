Experiment credentials

Here you can find the information of the experiment that you will need to interact with Organicity from your applications and software. You developers can find detailed information about how to use these credentials here .

Go To Credentials

Applications

Application are the parts of the experiment that users enjoy. In Organicity , one experiment can have several application so that you can differentiate which one is creating or consuming your experiment data. Here you can define your applications and obtain the information necessary for them to interact with the platform. In the assets tab you will find how to use the information of your applications.

Disclaimer! The meaning of application here is not the software, but an Organicity entity with an identifier. You will never be required to upload the code of your application.
Go To Applications

Assets

Create information for your experiment. From you will be able to create data assets for your experiment in a graphical way and you will get familiar with its structure and sintaxis. While it is a good starting point, it is not you but your application that will create the assets. Once you know how your assets looks like, here you will find how to create assets from your software.

Go To Assets

Annotations

Help people to enrich your information. Here you can define tags for your data assets, that will be displayed in the Urban Data Observatory so that people can select the ones that better fit the actual information. Tags are grouped in tag-domains, and only one tag of each domain can be selected. Here you will find a default domain for your experiment in which you can add tags. Besides, you can create more domains or select from Organicity default domains.

Go To Annotations

Area

Define the are of your experiment. By doing so ypu can limit the activity of the application of your experiment to those places yopu are interested in. From the Organicity perspective, this option is necessary to work with Smartphone Experimentation tool.

Go To Area

Experiment team

You do not need to work alone. Here you can invite other experiments to this experiment so that they will see all the experiment information.

Alert! Note that invited experimenters will see sensible information of your experiment (identifiers, experiment_secret, created information, etc.). Be sure that you really want to share this information.
